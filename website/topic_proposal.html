Chosen Topic Name:

Digital Simulation and Serious Gaming in education and learning

Chosen Topic Description:

Our group, consisting of Hyojun, Hussein, and Kyle, has chosen the topic of "Computers for education and learning". We all have experience playing computer games, and we believe that these games and other computer-based tools might be able to assist with education. Our society continues to become more technological, as schools in New Zealand use computer technology as a primary way of educating students. We plan to create a presentation that explores the different ways that computers are being used in education, such as the use of digital simulations and 'Serious Gaming'. We believe that these techniques of computer education will help younger students in learning faster and easier. We hope to provide you with a comprehensive overview of the topic, without getting too excited. So, if you're interested in learning more about the use of computers in education, stay tuned for our presentation.

Ethical Reflection

Both Serious Gaming and Digital Simulations have become more prominent to be acknowledged as a promising tool to promote both mental health and well-being. By using the engaging and associated nature of digital simulations and serious gaming, individuals would have the ability to improve their own mental well-being, managing their stress and developing coping mechanisms by using technologies that can provide opportunities for them.

One of the main issues is stress. It is said that both digital simulations and serious gaming have the capabilities to offer a beneficial way to offer both stress reduction and relaxation experiences. Ways to achieve this would be through exercise and audio elements, such examples come as audio from games and audio of natural sounds, it is said that Mood disorders such as depression and anxiety may have the capability to respond well to sound therapy Nyhus (2022, April 26). In one study a combined sound of singing bowls had the ability to decrease tension, anxiety and depression while promoting a growth of spiritual well being (Goldsby, 2017). Other ways to get rid of stress through serious gaming and digital simulations are through exercise. Albu et all., (n.d.) showed that with new developments into technology, the use of serious gaming and digital simulation is increasingly being accepted as many options in motivating people, and promoting them with positive health behaviours. The purpose of this study was to create a useful exercise simulation which they called BringItOn, which was the target to individuals who were in need to increase their physical activity, health and rehabilitation. It was then shown that from Albu et al., (n.d.) that three advantages of the use of simulations were shown, one showing how it promotes the proper exercise technique to the individual, individualised feedback as if it was from a real personal trainer, and finally that due to it being closely resembling a game made the exercise more fun. Fleming et al., (2016, December 28) has even reported that subjects that had improved mood after playing a casual game after handing them a frustrating task, which showed the students had improved their mood immediately, claiming that entertainment games were proposed to change the mood, emotional regulation, and stress released. One major positive of digital simulations and serious gaming is from stress relief, as games could help the individual relieve stress, where all the aggression, though playing games can be easily vented out resulting in decreased stress and healthy mood and mindset Jain, A. (2020, February 14).

However, It is also known that digital simulations and serious gaming have some flaws as well as there are chances of these systems being very addictive causing health problems such as depression and anxiety which would lead to stress. An addiction is a main point and primary aspect of life, including video games. The average time for an individual to play games, develop with programming, playing, testing, would take an average of 2-4 hours Team,. (2023, March 18).. Adair,. (2022, September 18). claims the the dopamine theory of addiction is a key root when playing video games due to the game sending quick releases of dopamine, but with too much dopamine it can cause an imbalance affecting our attitude which may lead to depression and anxiety, despite the aforementioned of positives of digital simulations and serious gaming, there are shown that excessive use of gaming can cause detrimental effects on the mental wellbeing. Adair,. (2022, September 18). Even claims there's the aspect of social isolation and gaming addiction. Due to most gaming addictions being deprived of the real world with social connections, etc, this would be the start to them feeling depressed which could lead to stress. Furthermore anxiety plays a role as well, where general anxiety and social anxiety are not too different. Where one the gamer takes a hold of video games, they are most likely to feel depression and anxiety and stress, which were set in stone throughout the beginning of the addiction Adair,. (2022, September 18).

Ethical Reflection

Both Serious Gaming and Digital Simulations have become more prominent to be acknowledged as a promising tool to promote both mental health and well-being. By using the engaging and associated nature of digital simulations and serious gaming, individuals would have the ability to improve their own mental well-being, managing their stress and developing coping mechanisms by using technologies that can provide opportunities for them. One of the main issues is stress.

It is said that both digital simulations and serious gaming have the capabilities to offer a beneficial way to offer both stress reduction and relaxation experiences. Ways to achieve this would be through exercise and audio elements, such examples come as audio from games and audio of natural sounds, it is said that Mood disorders such as depression and anxiety may have the capability to respond well to sound therapy Julie Nyhus (2022, April 26). In one study a combined sound of singing bowls had the ability to decrease tension, anxiety and depression while promoting a growth of spiritual well being (Goldsby, 2017). Other ways to get rid of stress through serious gaming and digital simulations are through exercise. Albu et all., (n.d.) showed that with new developments into technology, the use of serious gaming and digital simulation is increasingly being accepted as many options in motivating people, and promoting them with positive health behaviours. The purpose of this study was to create a useful exercise simulation which they called BringItOn, which was the target to individuals who were in need to increase their physical activity, health and rehabilitation. It was then shown that from Albu et al., (n.d.) that three advantages of the use of simulations were shown, one showing how it promotes the proper exercise technique to the individual, individualized feedback as if it was from a real personal trainer, and finally that due to it being closely resembling a game made the exercise more fun. Rossoniello et al., (2009) has even reported that subjects that had improved mood after playing a casual game after handing them a frustrating task, which showed the students had improved their mood immediately, claiming that entertainment games were proposed to change the mood, emotional regulation, and stress released.

However, it is also known that digital simulations and serious gaming have some flaws as well as there are chances of these systems being very costly , user interface issues. Cost considerations are a main point and primary aspect of life, including video games. The average cost for game development with programming, testing, marketing, this would still need to be passed down to the purchasers to sustain developers and publishers. Lewis, et al. (2020, April 15) claim the cost of video games can range to a mere few dollars to millions. One good Example would include Grand Theft Auto, where the game production cost $137.5 million dollars. With educational video games the cost would depend on key factors like simulation and graphics. Other examples include user interface issues. This type of software can be easily broken down into two categories. One having been the use of actual user interface issues surrounding the user and the second point being for platform distributions.

Examples of ways gaming can be used as a way for individuals who enjoy games and want a way to unwind themselves and remove stress to improve their health and well-being, this can be reflected on to me (Hussein) as I myself do find that playing games helps me de-stress from the work that is needed to be completed. Furthermore the use of games does help me go into a calm and relaxing environment as said before in the text. I also see Improvement in fine movement coordination with hands and Audio visual coordination, this helps me improve my reflexes when I play games, making me see and react to what's happening quicker. These skills that I get from using digital simulations and serious gaming allow me to be myself and enjoy the time without putting any extra weight on my shoulders. I see these factors benefit my skill and ability that not many people consider but allow me to feel confident in myself. I get to see vibrant colours designs and ways artist have put their work into the game, allowing me to get creative ideas and thoughts while i play and implement in the real world.

However I too feel the negative effects of Digital simulations and serious gaming. As overtime I have felt that from time to time serious gaming can get too distracting and time consuming as I would believe that I could have used that time playing games as an opportunity to finish some work or improve or create new projects with, unlike spending hours starting at a screen wasting my time instead of doing something beneficial, Furthermore there have been chances that I would play alone like many others and I see that as a way people socially isolate themselves, even though many days we can talk via call, it has been shown that talking to others face-to-face is more healthy.

And Finally the fact that Numbness plays a role. Sitting for hours not moving will hurt my body including everyone who tries it. This may cause harm to our bodies and cause negative effects to our health as well, an example of this may be Carpal tunnel syndrome where numbness and pain in the forearm and hand begin to occur.

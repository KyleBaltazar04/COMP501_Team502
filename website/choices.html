The choices of Digital Simulations and Serious Gaming

There are many choices to be made in adapting computers into education, with many techniques in Digital Simulations and Serious Gaming available to be implemented .

Computers play a pivotal role in education and learning, particularly in the context of digital simulations and serious gaming. They enable the creation and utilization of interactive and immersive simulations, offering experiential and hands-on learning experiences. Students can engage in scenario exploration, test hypotheses, and analyze the outcomes of their decisions within simulated environments. This active participation enhances critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making skills. Computers also facilitate collaborative learning, allowing students to work together, communicate effectively, and solve problems collectively within virtual environments. Additionally, computers capture and analyze data, providing personalized feedback and enabling instructors to tailor instruction to individual student needs. Computers enhance engagement, promote skill development, and empower students to acquire knowledge and competencies in an interactive and dynamic manner (University of Cumberland, 2023). With this idea of Digital simulations and serious gaming beginning the open entry to studies and research, it allows such students to advance with their skills as well allowing them to improve themselves and properly prepare them for their careers, such examples can be seen with activities like Flight Simulators.

The use of flight simulators is one of the main points when Digital Simulations and Serious gaming comes in. As in the name “Flight Simulator”, This Digital Simulation allows student and others to experience flying planes in the comfort of their screen. Furthermore the main functions Flight Simulator allows the stimulation of various aircrafts in various environments and conditions from rain, heavy wind, etc. Flight Simulator also known as Flight Training Device (FTD), Aviation Training Device and Full flight Simulator (FFS) allows the student to see the point of view of true flight and aviation, allowing them to do it in a much safer zone, compared to experiencing it in real life, unlike having to experience rough conditions where the danger and impracticality may harm them. Such an example may be air density, wind, clouds, rain, etc. The use of these simulations allow pilots to encounter real life situations which would allow them to practice in difficult and hard maneuvers. According to Epic Flight Academy.. (2021, December 13), it is said that students would be able to simulate flight at airports around the whole world, giving students the chance to encounter flights across the globe at any time of day. Furthermore, Epic Flight Academy.. (2021, December 13), claims that flight instructors have the ability to view the reactions of student pilots, specifically they have the chance to check students applications of “flight controls and effects of other aircraft systems”. It is also to mention that Epic Flight Academy provides safe training conditions.

This overall helps the student and society as it allows the use of implementations that would help them improve and gain a better understanding of their career and development. Furthermore, we see how society implements these techniques into the real world and digital world, some of the well known uses in society include :

Educational institutions such as Universities and colleges combine digital simulations and serious gaming into the students curriculum. Furthermore it would allow such educational institutions to set up dedicated computer labs or computer based learning tools and software. Also teachers can customize the lessons and classes that would allow students to involve themselves in interactive simulations and serious gaming elements to improve their students' engagement in the class while learning the outcomes.

Online learning platforms are another implementation as with the rise of online educational learning, both platforms and websites that are dedicated to education would have the ability in combine both digital simulations and serious gaming which would allow students to practice and use their knowledge to apply in a realistic environment.

Professional training programs are industries that would focus more on hands-on training, such as aviation, medical simulations and military training, where jobs such as these can used to train professionals. Having opportunities such as aviation, medical simulations and military training simulations would provide realistic scenarios to students and allow teachers to coordinate with them to see what they need to do or how to fix certain obstacles within their profession to provide realistic scenarios to students learning to practice and progress their skills.

With the use of Workshops and training Sessions, organizations and institutes would have the ability to organize training sessions and courses including workshops that would get focus to the learner on having to use digital simulations and serious gaming throughout their education in institutes, universities and college. These events would be provided to all people, such as educators, trainers, etc within their necessary knowledge skills to implement techniques into their everyday life in their respective fields.

Research and development would allow society to be able to invest into research and many development initiatives which would improve their digital simulations and serious gaming technologies. The use of research and development would allow collaborations that involve academic institutions, technology companies, and others to advance their capabilities and their effectiveness with the help of these tools. As with continuous innovation having the ability to lead into the development of more complex and sophisticated gaming and simulations experiences.

With society having the use of digital simulations and serious gaming incorporated into education, work, etc, society can implement these techniques by promoting and proposing collaborations and knowledge sharing. With companies, Educational Institutes and others investing in the development and integrations of the use of serious gaming and digital simulations in various sections it will allow the world to improve itself better in the long run with many having the advantage of perfecting their skills and speciality. As Society grows, so does the use of digital simulations and serious gaming helping teachers, students and other in improving their careers in a safe, healthy environment.
